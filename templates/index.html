How it works

Scan a QR code to stamp your passport. Collect all the required passport stamps and be entered to win! Winners will be announced at the Hair Ball on Tuesday night and notified via email.

Prizes:
  1. 1 complimentary registration for the 2027 MidWinter Meeting
  2. 1 complimentary year of ARO membership
How to Play:
  1. Input your information the first time you can scan a code. You won't need to again.
  2. Visit all {{ exhibitors_required }} of our exhibitors and scan each of their QR codes
  3. Attend all {{ sessions_required }} Passport Stop sessions and scan each QR code (Presidential Symposium, General Assembly, and the Awards Ceremony)
  4. Watch your progress update each time you scan a new QR code
  5. Complete your passport by 5:00PM local time on Tuesday, February 10th, 2026 to be entered to win!
Get Started: Look for QR codes at exhibitor booths and session rooms throughout the conference. Simply scan any QR code with your phone's camera to begin!
